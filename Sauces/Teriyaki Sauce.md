# Teriyaki Sauce

[From Here](https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/259887/simple-teriyaki-sauce/)

> This quick and easy homemade teriyaki sauce is perfect for meats, fish, stir-fries, and as a dipping sauce.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient          | Qty    | Unit  | Notes                                                |
|:--------------------|:------:|:-----:|:-----------------------------------------------------|
| Water               | 1      | cup   |                                                      |
| Soy sauce           | 1/4    | cup   |                                                      |
| Brown sugar         | 5      | tsp   | Packed                                               |
| Honey               | 1      | Tbsp  | Or more to taste                                     |
| Ground ginger       | 1/2    | tsp   |                                                      |
| Garlic powder       | 1/4    | tsp   |                                                      |
||||
| Cornstarch          | 2      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Cold water          | 1/4    | cup   | For slurry                                           |

## Instructions

1. **Heat Base:** Combine 1 cup water, soy sauce, brown sugar, honey, ginger, and garlic powder in a saucepan over medium heat. Cook until nearly heated through, about 1 minute.
2. **Prepare Slurry:** In a separate small bowl, mix the cornstarch with 1/4 cup cold water until completely dissolved.
3. **Thicken Sauce:** Add the cornstarch mixture to the saucepan. Cook and stir continuously until the sauce reaches your desired thickness, about 5 to 7 minutes.
4. **Cool:** Remove from heat and allow the sauce to cool to room temperature before using or storing in an airtight container.

**Notes:**

* **Storage:** Can be stored in the refrigerator for up to one week.
* **Versatility:** Excellent as a marinade, stir-fry sauce, or dipping sauce for wings and dumplings.
