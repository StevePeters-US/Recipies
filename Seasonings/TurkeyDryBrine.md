# Turkey Dry Brine


## Ingredients

| Type | Amount |
| --- | --- |
| Salt | 1/4 cup | 
| Brown Sugar | 2 Tbsp | 
| Garlic Powder | 1 Tbsp |
| Black Pepper | 1 Tbsp |
| Rosemary | 1 tsp | 
| Sage | 1 tsp |
<br/>
> Rub this on a broken down turkey the day before you cook it. Cook at 350f till thickest part of the breast is at 165f
