# White Chicken Chili

[From Here](https://tastesbetterfromscratch.com/creamy-white-chicken-chili/)

> A creamy, flavorful white bean chili that's healthy and easy to make on the stovetop, slow cooker, or Instant Pot.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient          | Qty    | Unit  | Notes                                                |
|:--------------------|:------:|:-----:|:-----------------------------------------------------|
| Olive oil           | 1      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Yellow onion        | 1      | small | Chopped (about 1/2 cup)                              |
| Garlic cloves       | 2      |       | Finely minced                                        |
||||
| Chicken broth       | 2 1/2  | cups  | Low-sodium                                           |
| Diced green chilies | 2      | cans  | 4 oz cans                                            |
| Ground cumin        | 1 1/2  | tsp   |                                                      |
| Cayenne pepper      | 1/4    | tsp   |                                                      |
| Dried oregano       | 1/2    | tsp   |                                                      |
| Paprika             | 1/2    | tsp   |                                                      |
| Lime                | 1/2    | small | Juice from                                           |
| Salt                |        |       | To taste                                             |
| Black pepper        |        |       | Freshly ground, to taste                             |
||||
| Great northern beans| 2      | cans  | 15 oz cans, drained and rinsed                       |
| Sour cream          | 1      | cup   | Or plain Greek yogurt                                |
| Corn                | 1      | cup   | Frozen or fresh                                      |
| Cooked chicken      | 2      | cups  | Heaping, shredded (rotisserie or leftover)           |

## Instructions

1. **Sauté:** Heat olive oil in a large (5-6 quart) pot over medium-high heat. Add onion and sauté until softened (3-5 minutes). Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
2. **Season:** Add chicken broth, green chilies, cumin, cayenne, paprika, oregano, lime juice, and salt and pepper to taste.
3. **Thicken:** Drain and rinse beans. Measure out a large ladleful of beans and puree with a splash of broth in a food processor until smooth. This step is optional but yields a creamier texture.
4. **Simmer:** Add pureed beans, whole beans, and corn to the pot. Bring to a simmer and cook, uncovered, for 15-30 minutes.
5. **Finish:** Remove from heat and stir in sour cream and cooked chicken.
6. **Serve:** Garnish with cilantro, shredded cheese, avocado slices, and tortilla chips if desired.

**Notes:**

* **Healthier Option:** Use Greek yogurt instead of sour cream for a lighter version with extra protein.
* **Using Raw Chicken:** If using raw chicken instead of rotisserie, add 1.5 lbs in step 2 with the broth and cook until tender.
* **Slow Cooker:** Add all ingredients except sour cream and cooked chicken to the slow cooker. Cook on low for 5 hours or high for 3 hours. Stir in sour cream and chicken at the end.
* **Instant Pot:** Sauté onion and garlic, then add all ingredients except sour cream. Cook on Manual/High Pressure for 8 minutes. Natural release for 15 minutes, then stir in sour cream.
