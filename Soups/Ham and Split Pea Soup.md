# Ham and Split Pea Soup

[From Here](https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/219170/ham-and-split-pea-soup-recipe-a-great-soup/)

> A hearty, classic split pea soup with a smoky ham flavor, perfect for a cold day.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient          | Qty    | Unit  | Notes                                                |
|:--------------------|:------:|:-----:|:-----------------------------------------------------|
| Butter              | 2      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Celery              | 2      | ribs  | Diced                                                |
| Onion               | 1/2    |       | Diced                                                |
| Garlic cloves       | 3      |       | Sliced                                               |
||||
| Dried split peas    | 1      | lb    | Rinsed                                               |
| Cooked ham          | 1      | lb    | Diced                                                |
| Bay leaf            | 1      |       |                                                      |
||||
| Chicken stock       | 1      | quart |                                                      |
| Water               | 2      | cups  |                                                      |
| Salt                |        |       | To taste                                             |
| Black pepper        |        |       | To taste                                             |

## Instructions

1. **Sauté:** Melt butter in a large soup pot over medium-low heat. Stir in celery, onion, and sliced garlic; cook and stir until onions are translucent but not brown, about 5 to 8 minutes.
2. **Combine:** Stir in split peas, ham, and bay leaf.
3. **Simmer:** Pour in chicken stock and water; stir to combine. Simmer until peas are tender and the soup has thickened, about 1 hour and 15 minutes. Stir occasionally.
4. **Season:** Season with salt and pepper to taste before serving.

**Notes:**

* **Prep/Cook Time:** Takes about 15 minutes to prep and 1.5 hours to cook.
