# Loaded Baked Potato Soup

[From Here](https://addapinch.com/baked-potato-soup-recipe/)

> Loaded baked potato soup makes a warm, comforting potato soup recipe. Made with baked potatoes blended into a creamy soup and topped with your potato bar favorites!

## Ingredients

| Ingredient          | Qty    | Unit  | Notes                                                |
|:--------------------|:------:|:-----:|:-----------------------------------------------------|
| Baked Potatoes      | 3 - 4  | med   |                                                      |
| Bacon               | 3 - 4  | slices| Diced, thick-cut recommended                         |
| All-purpose Flour   | 1/4    | cup   |                                                      |
| Milk                | 4      | cups  |                                                      |
| Chicken Stock       | 1      | cup   | Or broth                                             |
| Kosher Salt         | 1/2    | tsp   |                                                      |
||||
| Cheddar Cheese      |        |       | Grated, optional topping                             |
| Green Onions        |        |       | Sliced, optional topping                             |
| Caramelized Onions  |        |       | Optional topping                                     |
| Sour Cream          |        |       | Optional topping                                     |
| Chives              |        |       | Optional topping                                     |

## Instructions

1.  **Cook Bacon:** Dice bacon and cook over medium heat in at least a 3-quart Dutch oven until crisp and cooked throughout. With a slotted spoon, remove cooked bacon from the Dutch oven.
2.  **Make Roux:** Whisk the flour into the bacon renderings until well-combined but do not cook until browned.
3.  **Simmer Base:** Whisk in milk, stock, and salt. Continue stirring until soup has thickened and begins to bubble, about 12 minutes.
4.  **Add Potatoes:** Split baked potatoes in half and scoop insides of the baked potatoes into the thickened soup. Alternatively, peel the potatoes, give them a rough chop and add them into the soup.
5.  **Blend:** Using a potato masher or an immersion blender, incorporate the baked potatoes into the soup until well combined.
6.  **Serve:** Top with optional toppings and serve hot.

**Notes:**

* **Storage:** Store without the optional toppings in an airtight container for up to 3 days in the refrigerator or a freezer safe container for up to 1 month in the freezer.
