# Honey Garlic Chicken

[From Here](https://www.kitchensanctuary.com/honey-garlic-chicken/)

> Sticky, tender boneless chicken thighs in a garlic, soy, and honey sauce. Ready in just 20 minutes!

## Ingredients

| Ingredient          | Qty    | Unit  | Notes                                                |
|:--------------------|:------:|:-----:|:-----------------------------------------------------|
| Chicken thighs      | 8      |       | Skinless and boneless                                |
| Cornflour           | 2      | Tbsp  | Cornstarch                                           |
| Salt                | 1/2    | tsp   |                                                      |
| Pepper              | 1/2    | tsp   |                                                      |
| Vegetable oil       | 2      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Unsalted butter     | 1      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Garlic cloves       | 4      |       | Minced                                               |
||||
| Honey               | 1/3    | cup   | 110g                                                 |
| Chicken stock       | 1/3    | cup   | 80ml                                                 |
| Rice vinegar        | 1      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Light soy sauce     | 1      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
||||
| Fresh parsley       | 1      | Tbsp  | Finely chopped                                       |
| Chilli flakes       | 1/2    | tsp   |                                                      |
| Boiled rice         |        |       | For serving                                          |

## Instructions

1. **Coat Chicken:** Place the chicken thighs in a bowl and add the cornflour, salt, and pepper. Toss until fully coated.
2. **Sear:** Heat the vegetable oil in a large frying pan (skillet) over high heat. Add the chicken and cook on one side until golden brown (about 4-5 minutes). Turn over and cook for a further 2 minutes.
3. **Add Garlic:** Add the butter to the pan. Once melted, add the garlic and stir together. Turn the heat down to medium to prevent the garlic from burning.
4. **Make Sauce:** Combine the honey, stock, rice vinegar, and light soy sauce in a bowl and stir together.
5. **Simmer:** Add the sauce to the pan. Turn the heat up and bring the sauce to a boil, then simmer for 4-5 minutes until the sauce reduces and thickens and the chicken is cooked through.
6. **Serve:** Sprinkle with chopped parsley and chilli flakes. Serve immediately over boiled rice.

**Notes:**

* **Vinegar Swap:** If you don’t have rice vinegar, you can swap for apple cider vinegar or white wine vinegar plus 1/4 tsp of sugar.
* **Soy Sauce Swap:** If you don’t have light soy sauce, you can swap for 3/4 tbsp of dark soy sauce.
* **Chicken Thighs:** Thigh meat is recommended as it stays more tender than breast meat and handles high heat better without drying out.
