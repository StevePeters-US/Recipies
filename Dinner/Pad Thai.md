# Pad Thai

[From Here](https://tastesbetterfromscratch.com/pad-thai/)

> This easy Pad Thai recipe is made in just 30 minutes, and packed with bold flavor, and fresh ingredients. It's restaurant quality, amazing!

## Ingredients

| Ingredient          | Qty    | Unit  | Notes                                                |
|:--------------------|:------:|:-----:|:-----------------------------------------------------|
| Flat Rice Noodles   | 8      | oz    |                                                      |
| Oil                 | 3      | Tbsp  | divided                                              |
| Garlic              | 3      | cloves| Minced                                               |
| Protein             | 8      | oz    | Shrimp, chicken, or extra-firm tofu (small pieces)   |
| Eggs                | 2      |       |                                                      |
| Fresh Bean Sprouts  | 1      | cup   |                                                      |
| Red Bell Pepper     | 1      |       | Thinly sliced                                        |
| Green Onions        | 3      |       | Chopped                                              |
| Dry Roasted Peanuts | 1/2    | cup   |                                                      |
| Limes               | 2      |       |                                                      |
| Fresh Cilantro      | 1/2    | cup   | Chopped                                              |
||||
| Fish Sauce          | 3      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Low-sodium Soy Sauce| 1      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Light Brown Sugar   | 5      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Rice Vinegar        | 2      | Tbsp  | Or Tamarind Paste (see notes)                        |
| Sriracha Hot Sauce  | 1      | Tbsp  | Or more, to taste                                    |
| Creamy Peanut Butter| 2      | Tbsp  | Optional                                             |

## Instructions

1.  **Cook Noodles:** Cook noodles according to package instructions, just until tender. Rinse under cold water.
2.  **Make Sauce:** Make sauce by combining sauce ingredients (Fish sauce through Peanut butter) in a bowl. Set aside.
3.  **Stir Fry:** Heat 1 1/2 tablespoons of oil in a wok or large saucepan over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp, chicken or tofu, garlic and bell pepper.
    * *Shrimp:* Cook 1-2 minutes on each side until pink.
    * *Chicken:* Cook 3-4 minutes until just cooked through, flipping once.
4.  **Cook Eggs:** Push everything to the side of the pan. Add a little more oil and add the beaten eggs. Scramble the eggs, breaking them into small pieces with a spatula as they cook.
5.  **Combine:** Add noodles, sauce, bean sprouts and peanuts to the wok (reserving some peanuts for topping at the end). Toss everything to combine.
6.  **Serve:** Garnish the top with green onions, extra peanuts, cilantro and lime wedges. Serve immediately!

**Notes:**

* **Make Ahead:** Cut protein and chop veggies ahead of time. Stir sauce ingredients together and refrigerate until ready to use.
* **Tamarind Paste:** Substitute 2 Tablespoons Tamarind paste in place of the vinegar, for a more authentic sauce.
* **Vegan or Vegetarian:** Leave out the egg. Use tofu, and substitute the fish sauce for more soy sauce.
* **Peanut Butter:** Adds creaminess and a boost of flavor.
* **Storage:** Store leftovers in the fridge and enjoy within 2-3 days.
