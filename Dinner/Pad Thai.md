# Pad Thai

[From Here](https://tastesbetterfromscratch.com/pad-thai/)

> This easy Pad Thai recipe is made in just 30 minutes and packed with bold flavor and fresh ingredients.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient          | Qty    | Unit  | Notes                                                |
|:--------------------|:------:|:-----:|:-----------------------------------------------------|
| Flat rice noodles   | 8      | oz    |                                                      |
| Oil                 | 3      | Tbsp  | Divided                                              |
| Garlic cloves       | 3      |       | Minced                                               |
| Shrimp, chicken, or tofu | 8 | oz    | Cut into small pieces                                |
| Eggs                | 2      |       | Beaten                                               |
| Bean sprouts        | 1      | cup   | Fresh                                                |
| Red bell pepper     | 1      |       | Thinly sliced                                        |
| Green onions        | 3      |       | Chopped                                              |
| Peanuts             | 1/2    | cup   | Dry roasted, divided                                 |
| Limes               | 2      |       | Cut into wedges                                      |
| Fresh cilantro      | 1/2    | cup   | Chopped                                              |
||||
| Fish sauce          | 3      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Low-sodium soy sauce| 1      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Light brown sugar   | 5      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Rice vinegar        | 2      | Tbsp  | Or tamarind paste                                    |
| Sriracha            | 1      | Tbsp  | Or more to taste                                     |
| Creamy peanut butter| 2      | Tbsp  | Optional                                             |

## Instructions

1. **Prepare Noodles:** Cook noodles according to package instructions just until tender. Rinse under cold water.
2. **Make Sauce:** Combine fish sauce, soy sauce, brown sugar, rice vinegar, Sriracha, and peanut butter (if using) in a bowl. Set aside.
3. **Stir Fry:** Heat 1 1/2 tablespoons of oil in a wok or large saucepan over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp, chicken, or tofu, garlic, and bell pepper. Cook until the protein is just cooked through.
4. **Scramble Eggs:** Push everything to the side of the pan. Add a little more oil and the beaten eggs. Scramble until cooked, breaking them into small pieces with a spatula.
5. **Combine:** Add noodles, sauce, bean sprouts, and half of the peanuts to the pan. Toss everything to combine.
6. **Serve:** Garnish with green onions, extra peanuts, cilantro, and lime wedges. Serve immediately.

**Notes:**

* **Tamarind Paste:** Substitute 2 tablespoons of tamarind paste for the rice vinegar for a more authentic sauce.
* **Vegan/Vegetarian:** Leave out the eggs, use tofu, and substitute the fish sauce with more soy sauce.
* **Peanut Butter:** Adding peanut butter to the sauce adds creaminess and a boost of flavor.
