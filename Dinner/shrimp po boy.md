# Shrimp Po Boy

[From Here](https://www.favfamilyrecipes.com/shrimp-po-boy-recipe/)

> Shrimp Po Boy is the ultimate sandwich for seafood lovers! Tender shrimp drizzled in a creamy, spicy sauce and topped with crunchy cabbage on a soft roll, it is the perfect balance of flavor, texture, and color.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient          | Qty    | Unit  | Notes                                                |
|:--------------------|:------:|:-----:|:-----------------------------------------------------|
| Mayonnaise          | 1      | cup   | Best Foods or Hellmann's                             |
| Ketchup             | 2/3    | cup   |                                                      |
| Horseradish Sauce   | 3      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Lemon Juice         | 1      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Creole Seasoning    | 1/2 - 1| Tbsp  | Depending on spice preference (for sauce)            |
| Garlic Powder       | 1/2    | tsp   |                                                      |
| Paprika             | 1/2    | tsp   |                                                      |
||||
| Butter              | 4      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Garlic              | 2      | cloves| Minced                                               |
| Shrimp              | 1      | lb    | Medium to large, uncooked, peeled and deveined       |
| Creole Seasoning    |        |       | To taste (for shrimp)                                |
| Hoagie Rolls        | 4      |       | French-style or French bread                         |
| Shredded Cabbage    |        |       | Or lettuce                                           |

## Instructions

1.  **Make Sauce:** Combine sauce ingredients (Mayonnaise through Paprika) in a mixing bowl until smooth. Set aside.
2.  **Sauté Aromatics:** Heat a large skillet to medium heat, add butter and garlic.
3.  **Cook Shrimp:** When the butter is hot and melted, add shrimp. Sprinkle Creole seasoning over the shrimp. It can be a little spicy so only sprinkle it to your personal taste. When the shrimp is pink all the way through, remove it from heat. Do not overcook.
4.  **Assemble:** Split the rolls open and divide the shrimp evenly between each roll.
5.  **Serve:** Top with the creamy Cajun sauce and shredded cabbage, to taste.

**Notes:**

* **Breaded Option:** If you like breaded and deep-fried shrimp, bread it in cornmeal and flour mixture, then fry in hot vegetable oil until golden brown.
* **Grilled Option:** Season shrimp with Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, cayenne, salt, and onion powder, then grill over medium-high heat.
* **Substitutions:** Replace shrimp with fried catfish, oysters, soft-shell crab, or lobster if desired.
* **Toppings:** Traditional toppings include shredded lettuce/cabbage, sliced tomatoes, pickles, mayonnaise, hot sauce, sliced onions, coleslaw, or jalapeño peppers.
