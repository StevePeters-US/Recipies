# Chicken Lettuce Wraps

[From Here](https://joyfoodsunshine.com/asian-chicken-lettuce-wraps/)

> A quick and healthy dinner that tastes even better than the restaurant version. Ready in less than 30 minutes!

## Ingredients

| Ingredient          | Qty    | Unit  | Notes                                                |
|:--------------------|:------:|:-----:|:-----------------------------------------------------|
| Ground chicken      | 1      | pound |                                                      |
| Peanut oil          | 1      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Onion               | 1/2    |       | Finely diced                                         |
| Minced garlic       | 1/2    | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Bell pepper         | 1      | cup   | Finely diced                                         |
| Water chestnuts     | 8      | ounces| Drained & finely diced                               |
||||
| Soy sauce           | 3      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Hoisin sauce        | 3      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Sesame oil          | 1      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Rice vinegar        | 1      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Peanut butter       | 1      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Honey               | 1      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Sweet chili sauce   | 2      | tsp   |                                                      |
| Garlic powder       | 1/2    | tsp   |                                                      |
| Powdered ginger     | 1/4    | tsp   |                                                      |
||||
| Peanuts             | 1/4    | cup   | Crushed, for serving                                 |
| Green onions        | 1/4    | cup   | Sliced, for serving                                  |
| Lettuce             |        |       | Butterhead, green leaf, or romaine                   |

## Instructions

1. **Whisk Sauce:** In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, peanut butter, honey, sweet chili sauce, garlic powder, and powdered ginger until well combined. If the peanut butter is firm, microwave for 30-60 seconds to soften.
2. **Sauté Chicken:** Heat the peanut oil in a large frying pan or wok. Once hot, add the ground chicken, onion, and minced garlic. Cook until the chicken is browned and the onions are translucent (5-10 minutes).
3. **Add Vegetables:** Add the diced bell peppers and water chestnuts. Cook for another 5 minutes or until the peppers are slightly soft.
4. **Simmer:** Pour the sauce over the chicken and vegetable mixture. Simmer on low heat until everything is evenly coated and heated through.
5. **Serve:** Spoon the filling into lettuce leaves. Garnish with crushed peanuts and sliced green onions.

**Notes:**

* **Chicken Substitutes:** Finely diced chicken breasts, ground turkey, or ground pork are great alternatives.
* **Gluten-Free:** Use tamari and ensure your hoisin sauce is labeled gluten-free.
* **Oil:** Olive or avocado oil can be used instead of peanut oil.
