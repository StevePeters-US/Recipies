# Shrimp Po Boy Sandwich

[From Here](https://www.simplyrecipes.com/recipes/shrimp_po_boy_sandwich/)

> A classic Louisiana po' boy featuring crispy fried shrimp, shredded lettuce, tomato, and a homemade remoulade sauce.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient          | Qty    | Unit  | Notes                                                |
|:--------------------|:------:|:-----:|:-----------------------------------------------------|
| Mayonnaise          | 1 1/4  | cups  |                                                      |
| Mustard             | 1/4    | cup   | Preferably Creole mustard                            |
| Prepared horseradish| 2      | tsp   |                                                      |
| Pickle juice        | 1      | tsp   | Or vinegar                                           |
| Hot sauce           | 1      | tsp   | Such as Crystal or Tabasco                           |
| Garlic clove        | 1      | large | Minced and smashed                                   |
| Sweet paprika       | 1      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Cajun seasoning     | 1-2    | tsp   |                                                      |
||||
| Medium shrimp       | 1      | lb    | Shelled, deveined, tails removed                     |
| Fine cornmeal       | 3/4    | cup   |                                                      |
| All-purpose flour   | 3/4    | cup   |                                                      |
| Cajun seasoning     | 1      | Tbsp  |                                                      |
| Salt                | 1      | tsp   |                                                      |
| Eggs                | 2      |       | Beaten                                               |
| Peanut oil          |        |       | For frying                                           |
||||
| Iceberg lettuce     | 1/2    | head  | Shredded                                             |
| Tomatoes            | 2-3    |       | Sliced 1/4 inch thick                                |
| French rolls        | 4      | small |                                                      |

## Instructions

1. **Prepare Remoulade:** Mix mayonnaise, mustard, horseradish, pickle juice, hot sauce, garlic, paprika, and 1-2 tsp Cajun seasoning in a bowl. Set aside for 30 minutes to meld.
2. **Heat Oil:** Pour peanut oil into a large frying pan to a depth of 1/4 inch. Heat over medium-high heat until a pinch of flour sizzles immediately.
3. **Breading Station:** In a large bowl, whisk together cornmeal, flour, 1 Tbsp Cajun seasoning, and salt.
4. **Coat Shrimp:** Dredge shrimp in beaten eggs, then coat thoroughly in the cornmeal mixture. Shake off excess.
5. **Fry:** Fry shrimp in batches until golden brown (about 2 minutes total). Drain on paper towels.
6. **Assemble:** Slice French rolls almost all the way through. Smear remoulade generously on both top and bottom.
7. **Layer:** Add shredded lettuce to the bottom, then arrange fried shrimp on top.
8. **Finish:** Place 3-4 tomato slices over the shrimp and press the top down slightly. Serve with extra hot sauce.

**Notes:**

* **Remoulade Prep:** Making the sauce ahead of time allows the flavors to develop significantly.
