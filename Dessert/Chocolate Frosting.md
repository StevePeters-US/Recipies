# Chocolate Frosting

## Ingredients
| Ingredient             | Qty    | Unit     | Notes       |
|:-----------------------|:------:|:--------:|:------------|
| Butter      | 1/2    | cup      |     Softened        |
| Cocoa powder           | 1/4    | cup      |             |
| Powdered sugar         | 2 1/2  | cups     |             |
| Vanilla                | 1/2    | tsp      |             |
| Milk                   | 1 - 2  | tbsp     |             |
                                                           

## Instructions

Whip butter and cocoa

Mix in vanilla and powdered sugar

Slowly add milk

Whip until light and fluffy
