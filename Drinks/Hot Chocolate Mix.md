# Hot Chocolate Mix

[From Here](https://foodwishes.blogspot.com/2015/12/homemade-instant-hot-chocolate-mix.html)

> A simple, four-ingredient cocoa mix that's much better than store-bought. A pinch of cayenne adds a subtle depth of flavor.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient          | Qty    | Unit  | Notes                                                |
|:--------------------|:------:|:-----:|:-----------------------------------------------------|
| Cocoa powder        | 1      | cup   | Dutch-processed recommended                          |
| White sugar         | 1 1/2  | cups  |                                                      |
| Fine salt           | 1      | tsp   |                                                      |
| Cayenne pepper      | 1      | pinch |                                                      |

## Instructions

1. **Combine:** In a medium bowl, combine the cocoa powder, sugar, salt, and cayenne pepper.
2. **Whisk:** Whisk the ingredients together thoroughly to remove any lumps and ensure the salt and cayenne are evenly distributed.
3. **Store:** Transfer the mixture to an airtight container or jar for storage.
4. **Prepare:** To make a cup of hot chocolate, stir 2 tablespoons of the mix into 1 cup (8 oz) of hot milk until completely dissolved.

**Notes:**

* **Serving Suggestion:** Enjoy as is, or top with marshmallows or whipped cream.
* **Storage:** Keep in a cool, dry place in an airtight container.
