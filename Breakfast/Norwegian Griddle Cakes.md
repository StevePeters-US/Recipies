# Norwegian Griddle Cakes with Buttercream (Sveler)

[From Here](https://northwildkitchen.com/norwegian-flat-cakes-with-buttercream-sveler/)

> Sveler are traditional, soft griddle cakes similar to an American pancake, often associated with ferry travel in Norway. They are best served with a slice of brown cheese or a good smearing of sweet buttercream.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient          | Qty    | Unit  | Notes                                                |
|:--------------------|:------:|:-----:|:-----------------------------------------------------|
| Eggs                | 3      | large | At room temperature                                  |
| Granulated Sugar    | 3/4    | cup   | (150 g) For cakes                                    |
| Butter              | 1/2    | cup   | (112 g) Melted, for cakes                            |
| Buttermilk          | 3      | cups  | (720 ml) Or kefir                                    |
| All-purpose Flour   | 3      | cups  | (360 g)                                              |
| Baking Soda         | 1      | tsp   |                                                      |
||||
| Butter              | 1/2    | cup   | (112 g) Softened, for buttercream                    |
| Confectioners' Sugar| 2/3    | cup   | (80 g) For buttercream                               |
| Granulated Sugar    | 5      | Tbsp  | For buttercream                                      |

## Instructions

1.  **Whisk Wet Ingredients:** In a large bowl, combine the eggs and sugar (3/4 cup) and whisk until fluffy. Whisk in the melted butter, followed by the buttermilk/kefir.
2.  **Add Dry Ingredients:** Combine the flour and baking soda, add to the batter, and stir gently until combined.
3.  **Rest:** Let stand for about 20 minutes—the dough will swell.
4.  **Heat Griddle:** Heat a takke to medium heat or place a large frying pan directly on the stove over medium heat, and lightly butter.
5.  **Cook Cakes:** Ladle some batter onto the hot takke or pan to make several 6-inch (15 cm) round griddle cakes. Cook for 2 minutes or until bubbles appear on top and the bottoms turn golden brown.
6.  **Flip:** Flip the griddle cakes and cook for 2 minutes more or until the other side is golden brown. Transfer to a plate and keep warm. Continue making griddle cakes with remaining batter.
7.  **Make Buttercream:** While the griddle cakes are cooling, make the buttercream by combining the softened butter, confectioners' sugar, and granulated sugar (5 Tbsp) in a bowl.
8.  **Serve:** Serve the cakes with the buttercream.

**Notes:**

* **Yield:** Makes about 18 sveler.
* **Toppings:** Can also be served with fresh berries, jams, sour cream, brown cheese, or honey.
* **Storage:** Store leftovers in an airtight container or cover with foil and refrigerate for up to 3 days. Freezes well.
